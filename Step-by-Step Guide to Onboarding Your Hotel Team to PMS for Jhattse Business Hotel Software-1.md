## 📋 Phase 1: Pre-Onboarding Preparation (Week -2 to -1)

### ✅ Step 1: Form Your "PMS Champion" Team
Identify 2-3 tech-comfortable staff members from different departments:
- Front Desk Supervisor
- Housekeeping Lead
- Restaurant/F&B Manager

These champions will:
- Attend advanced training first
- Serve as go-to peers during rollout
- Provide feedback to refine workflows

🔹 *Jhattse Tip*: We provide dedicated "Champion Training" sessions at no extra cost.

### ✅ Step 2: Audit & Clean Your Data
Before migration, ensure your master data is accurate:
- Room types, rates, and inventory
- Guest history (if migrating)
- Staff roles and permissions
- Restaurant menu items & pricing

🔹 *Jhattse Advantage*: Our migration support team helps import CSV files or legacy system exports—free of charge.

### ✅ Step 3: Customize Role-Based Access
Not everyone needs access to everything. Use Jhattse's permission settings to:
- Front Desk: Check-in/out, billing, guest profiles
- Housekeeping: Room status updates, task lists
- Managers: Reports, rate management, user admin
- Restaurant Staff: POS orders, table management

🔹 *Why it matters*: Reduces confusion, enhances security, and speeds up training.

### ✅ Step 4: Prepare Your Physical & Digital Workspace
- Ensure stable internet at front desk, kitchen, and housekeeping stations
- Install Jhattse on tablets/desktops (or use our cloud web app)
- Print quick-reference cheat sheets (we provide templates)

---

## 🎓 Phase 2: Role-Based Training (Week 0)

### ✅ Step 5: Conduct Department-Specific Workshops
Avoid "one-size-fits-all" training. Run focused 60-90 minute sessions:

| Department | Key Jhattse Modules | Hands-On Practice |
|-----------|-------------------|------------------|
| **Front Desk** | PMS Dashboard, Check-in/out, Billing, WhatsApp CRM | Simulate a peak-hour check-in with group booking |
| **Housekeeping** | Task Board, Room Status Sync, Mobile Alerts | Update room status from "Dirty" → "Clean" → "Inspected" |
| **Restaurant** | POS, Table Management, Order-to-Folio Sync | Place a room service order that posts to guest bill |
| **Management** | Reports, Channel Manager, Rate Calendar | Adjust OTA rates and see real-time sync |

🔹 *Jhattse Edge*: All training includes **Hindi + English** video tutorials, live demos, and sandbox practice environments.

### ✅ Step 6: Teach the "Guest Journey" Workflow
Help staff see how their role connects to the bigger picture:

```
Guest Books Online 
→ Channel Manager updates inventory 
→ Front Desk receives pre-arrival alert 
→ Housekeeping gets room assignment 
→ Guest checks in via WhatsApp 
→ Restaurant POS links dinner to folio 
→ Post-stay review request auto-sent
```

When teams understand the flow, they collaborate better—and spot issues faster.

### ✅ Step 7: Run a "Soft Launch" Pilot
Before going live hotel-wide:
- Use Jhattse for **new bookings only** for 2-3 days
- Keep legacy system as backup (read-only)
- Champions provide real-time support
- Collect feedback daily via quick huddles

🔹 *Pro Tip*: Start with low-occupancy days to reduce pressure.

---

## 🚀 Phase 3: Go-Live & Hypercare Support (Week +1)

### ✅ Step 8: Launch with "War Room" Support
On Day 1 of full rollout:
- Jhattse support team on standby via call/WhatsApp
- Champions stationed at key points (front desk, kitchen)
- Manager available to resolve escalations instantly

🔹 *Jhattse Promise*: Our India-based support team responds to critical issues within **15 minutes** during business hours.

### ✅ Step 9: Use In-App Guidance & Quick Help
Jhattse includes:
- Interactive tooltips for first-time users
- "Help" button with video snippets for common tasks
- Searchable knowledge base (mobile-friendly)

Encourage staff to use these *before* asking for help—building self-reliance.

### ✅ Step 10: Daily 10-Minute Huddles
For the first week, hold brief stand-ups:
- What worked well today?
- What felt confusing?
- Any guest feedback on the new process?

Document insights and share quick fixes immediately.

---

## 🔄 Phase 4: Post-Launch Optimization (Week +2 Onward)

### ✅ Step 11: Review Analytics & Refine Workflows
After 2 weeks, use Jhattse reports to:
- Identify bottlenecks (e.g., average check-in time)
- Spot training gaps (e.g., frequent "help" clicks on billing)
- Celebrate wins (e.g., 20% faster room turnover)

🔹 *Jhattse Edge*: Customizable dashboards show team-specific KPIs—no data science degree required.

### ✅ Step 12: Launch "Power User" Advanced Training
Once basics are mastered, offer optional modules:
- Upselling techniques using guest preference data
- Managing group bookings & corporate contracts
- Using WhatsApp CRM for personalized promotions

🔹 *Result*: Staff feel invested in—and become advocates for—the system.

### ✅ Step 13: Establish a Feedback Loop
Create a simple channel for ongoing suggestions:
- Monthly "Tech Tip" sharing in team meetings
- Digital suggestion box via WhatsApp
- Quarterly review with Jhattse success manager

Continuous improvement keeps adoption high.

## 🚫 Common Onboarding Pitfalls (and How to Avoid Them)

| Pitfall | Solution |
|--------|----------|
| **Training everyone at once** | Use role-based, staggered sessions |
| **Skipping the pilot phase** | Always test with new bookings first |
| **No post-launch support** | Schedule hypercare + champion check-ins |
| **Ignoring change resistance** | Involve staff early; highlight "What's in it for me?" |
| **Over-customizing upfront** | Start with best-practice workflows; tweak later |
