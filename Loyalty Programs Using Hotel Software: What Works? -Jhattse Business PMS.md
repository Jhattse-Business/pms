## 🎯 Types of Loyalty Programs That Work for Indian Hotels

Not all loyalty programs are created equal. Here are the **most effective models** for small and boutique hotels:

### Program #1: Points-Based Rewards

**How It Works**: Guests earn points per rupee spent, redeemable for discounts, upgrades, or free nights.

| Spend | Points Earned | Redemption Value |
|-------|--------------|------------------|
| ₹1,000 | 100 points | ₹100 off future stay |
| ₹5,000 | 500 points | ₹500 off or free breakfast |
| ₹10,000 | 1,000 points | Free night (up to ₹5,000 value) |

**Best For**: Properties with high repeat guest potential (city hotels, business destinations)

**Expected ROI**: 30-40% increase in repeat bookings

🔹 *Jhattse Feature*: Automated points calculation and redemption tracking within guest profiles.

---

### Program #2: Tier-Based Membership

**How It Works**: Guests unlock better benefits as they reach higher tiers.

| Tier | Qualification | Benefits |
|------|--------------|----------|
| **Silver** | 2+ stays/year | 5% discount, late checkout |
| **Gold** | 5+ stays/year | 10% discount, room upgrade, welcome drink |
| **Platinum** | 10+ stays/year | 15% discount, guaranteed upgrade, free breakfast, birthday gift |

**Best For**: Boutique hotels, heritage properties, resort destinations

**Expected ROI**: 40-50% increase in guest lifetime value

🔹 *Jhattse Feature*: Automatic tier upgrades based on stay history with benefit tracking.

---

### Program #3: Paid Membership (Subscription)

**How It Works**: Guests pay annual fee for exclusive benefits.

| Membership | Annual Fee | Benefits |
|-----------|-----------|----------|
| **Insider** | ₹2,999/year | 10% off all stays, free breakfast, priority booking |
| **Elite** | ₹4,999/year | 15% off, guaranteed upgrade, free spa credit, flexible cancellation |

**Best For**: Properties with strong brand loyalty and frequent travelers

**Expected ROI**: Upfront revenue + 50-60% higher booking frequency

🔹 *Jhattse Feature*: Membership management with automatic benefit application at booking.

---

### Program #4: Stay-Based Rewards

**How It Works**: Free night after X number of paid stays.

| Stays | Reward |
|-------|--------|
| 5 nights | 1 free night (up to 50% of average rate) |
| 10 nights | 2 free nights + room upgrade |
| 20 nights | 5 free nights + VIP status |

**Best For**: Extended-stay properties, serviced apartments, homestays

**Expected ROI**: 35-45% increase in booking frequency

🔹 *Jhattse Feature*: Stay counter tracking with automated reward notifications.

| Friend completes stay | ₹500 credit for referee |
| 5+ successful referrals | Free weekend stay |

**Best For**: Properties with strong word-of-mouth potential

**Expected ROI**: 25-35% of new bookings from referrals

## 🧭 Step-by-Step: Launching Your Loyalty Program

### ✅ Step 1: Define Your Goals
What do you want to achieve?

| Goal | Best Program Type |
|------|------------------|
| Increase repeat bookings | Points-Based or Stay-Based |
| Boost direct bookings | Tier-Based with direct-booking bonuses |
| Acquire new guests | Referral Program |
| Increase guest spend | Tier-Based with spend thresholds |
| Build brand advocacy | Occasion-Based + Referral combination |

🔹 *Jhattse Tip*: Start with ONE primary goal. Don't try to do everything at once.

---

### ✅ Step 2: Choose Your Program Structure
Based on your goals, select the right model:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│              LOYALTY PROGRAM DECISION TREE                      │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  High repeat guest potential? → YES → Points-Based or Tier     │
│                              → NO  → Referral or Occasion      │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  Average stay value >₹5,000? → YES → Tier-Based with upgrades  │
│                              → NO  → Points-Based with discounts│
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  Strong word-of-mouth? → YES → Referral Program                │
│                        → NO  → Focus on retention first        │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

🔹 *Jhattse Tip*: Most Indian hotels succeed with a hybrid: Tier-Based + Occasion triggers.

---

### ✅ Step 3: Set Your Reward Economics
Ensure your program is profitable:

| Reward Type | Cost to Hotel | Perceived Value | Recommended Ratio |
|------------|--------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Room Discount | Direct revenue loss | High | Max 15-20% |
| Room Upgrade | Low (if available) | High | Highly recommended |
| Free Breakfast | ₹200-400/guest | Medium-High | Great value-add |
| Free Night | One night revenue | Very High | Limit to top tiers |
| Spa/Experience Credit | Partner cost or marginal | High | Excellent for packages |
| Late Checkout | Zero (if available) | Medium | Always include |

**Golden Rule**: Reward cost should be 30-50% of the incremental revenue generated.

## 🚫 Common Loyalty Program Mistakes to Avoid

| Mistake | Why It Hurts | Better Approach |
|---------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Too complex to understand** | Guests don't enroll or participate | Keep it simple: earn X, get Y |
| **Rewards too hard to redeem** | Frustration, low engagement | Make redemption automatic at booking |
| **No expiration policy** | Liability buildup, unclear urgency | Set 12-24 month point expiration |
| **Benefits not valuable** | Low enrollment and engagement | Survey guests on desired rewards |
| **Poor communication** | Guests forget they're members | Automated WhatsApp updates monthly |
| **Disconnected from PMS** | Manual tracking, errors, frustration | Choose integrated software like Jhattse |
| **Launching without promotion** | Low awareness, slow adoption | Invest in launch campaign |
| **Not measuring ROI** | Can't optimize or justify investment | Track all loyalty KPIs in dashboard |
