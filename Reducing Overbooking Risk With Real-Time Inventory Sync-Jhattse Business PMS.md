**Overbooking** happens when you accept more reservations than your actual room capacity—usually because inventory wasn't updated across all sales channels in real time.

- Website bookings update everywhere instantly

No more switching between 5 different portals.

### ✅ 3. Smart Conflict Resolution
What if two bookings come in at the *exact* same second? Jhattse's system:
- Timestamps every booking to the millisecond
- Automatically accepts the first booking
- Instantly closes inventory on all other channels
- Sends alert to management for review

### ✅ 4. Automated Room Blocking
When a room goes out of service (maintenance, deep cleaning, etc.):
- Housekeeping marks it "Out of Order" in the app
- System automatically blocks it across all OTAs
- Room reopens automatically when status changes back

No manual updates. No forgotten blocks.

### ✅ 5. Buffer & Allocation Controls
Set smart rules to add extra protection:
- **Buffer rooms**: Keep 1-2 rooms unlisted on OTAs for walk-ins
- **Allocation limits**: Cap how many rooms each OTA can sell
- **Cut-off times**: Stop OTA sales 2-4 hours before check-in
