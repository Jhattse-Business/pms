## 🚨 The Problem: Disconnected Website and PMS

Many small hotels have a website with a booking form—but it's not integrated with their PMS. This creates major problems:

### ❌ Common Pain Points:

| Issue | Impact on Direct Bookings |
|-------|--------------------------|
| **Manual booking entry** | Staff must copy website bookings into PMS—time-consuming and error-prone |
| **Inventory mismatches** | Website shows available rooms that are already booked on OTAs |
| **Rate inconsistency** | Website rates don't match current PMS pricing |
| **No guest profile creation** | Direct booking data doesn't populate guest history |
| **Delayed confirmation** | Guests wait hours for booking confirmation instead of instant |
| **Payment reconciliation issues** | Website payments don't auto-match with PMS folios |

**Result**: Frustrated guests, double bookings, staff burnout, and low direct booking conversion.

**Impact**: 20-30% increase in direct booking inquiries.

**Impact**: 25-35% higher conversion on direct channel.
