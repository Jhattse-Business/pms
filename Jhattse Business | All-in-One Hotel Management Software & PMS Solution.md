## 🚀 Key Features & Capabilities

Jhattse Business provides a modular ecosystem covering every aspect of hotel operations.

### 1. Property Management System (PMS)
Centralize your front-desk operations. Manage check-ins, check-outs, room allocation, and guest profiles from a single dashboard.
*   **Benefit:** Reduces manual errors and speeds up guest processing.

### 2. Multi-Channel Manager
Synchronize room inventory and rates in real-time across all major Online Travel Agencies (OTAs) like Booking.com, Agoda, Expedia, and MakeMyTrip.
*   **Benefit:** Prevents overbookings and ensures rate parity across all platforms.

### 3. Direct Booking Engine
A commission-free booking widget integrated directly into your hotel website.
*   **Benefit:** Increases profit margins by encouraging direct reservations.

### 4. Restaurant POS Integration
Manage food and beverage orders with a Point of Sale system that links directly to guest room bills.
*   **Benefit:** Seamless billing experience for in-house guests.

### 5. WhatsApp Business API Integration
Automate guest communication including booking confirmations, pre-arrival messages, and support queries via WhatsApp.
*   **Benefit:** Improves guest satisfaction and response times.

### 6. Housekeeping Management
Digital task allocation for cleaning staff with real-time room status updates (Dirty, Clean, Inspected).
*   **Benefit:** Optimizes room turnover time and improves coordination.

### 7. Secure Payment Gateway
Integrated payment processing for secure online transactions and deposits.
*   **Benefit:** Enhances security and simplifies reconciliation.
