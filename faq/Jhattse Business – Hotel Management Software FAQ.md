## üìö Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. What is the Jhattse Business Hotel Management System (PMS)?

Our PMS is a robust **Front Desk solution** that acts as the heart of your hotel operations. It allows you to:

* Manage check-ins and check-outs instantly.
* View a real-time dashboard of room occupancy.
* Handle guest data securely.
* Streamline daily administrative tasks without paperwork.

### 2. How does the Channel Manager help prevent overbookings?

The **Jhattse Channel Manager** offers **real-time rate and inventory synchronization**. When a room is booked on one platform (like Booking.com or MakeMyTrip), our system instantly updates your inventory across all other channels and your own front desk. This eliminates double bookings and ensures your rates are always consistent.

### 3. Can I manage my hotel restaurant and billing with this software?

Yes! We include a dedicated **Restaurant POS (Point of Sale)** designed for efficient order and billing workflows.

* **Touch-friendly interface** for quick order taking.
* **Direct-to-Room Billing:** Guests can have their food bills added directly to their room folio for a single payment at checkout.
* **KOT Management:** Streamlines communication between the service staff and the kitchen.

### 4. How does the WhatsApp Business Integration work?

We understand that guests prefer instant communication. Our **WhatsApp Business Integration** allows for **direct guest interaction**, enabling you to:

* Send automated booking confirmations.
* Share invoices and payment links instantly.
* Respond to guest queries in real-time, improving customer satisfaction.

### 5. Does the software help with Housekeeping management?

Absolutely. Jhattse Business features **Smart Task Coordination** for housekeeping.

* **Automated Alerts:** When a guest checks out, the room status automatically changes to "Dirty."
* **Task Assignment:** Managers can assign specific cleaning tasks to staff members.
* **Real-time Updates:** Front desk staff can see immediately when a room is "Clean" and ready for the next guest.

### 6. Can I accept direct bookings without paying OTA commissions?

Yes. We provide a seamless **Booking Engine** that plugs directly into your website or social media. This allows guests to check availability and make **direct reservations** with you, helping you save significantly on third-party commission fees.

### 7. Is online payment collection secure?

We prioritize security. Our system comes with **Payment Gateway Integration** that supports secure online payments. You can collect booking deposits, settle bills, and track all transactions safely through the platform.
