## 🧭 How to Evaluate New Technology for Your Hotel

Not every trend is right for every property. Use this framework:

### ✅ The 5-Question Tech Evaluation Checklist:

| Question | What to Look For |
|----------|-----------------|
| **1. Does it solve a real problem?** | Avoid "nice to have" features; focus on pain points |
| **2. Is it compatible with existing systems?** | Prioritize native integrations over add-ons |
| **3. What's the true cost of ownership?** | Include setup, training, support—not just subscription |
| **4. Is it scalable for growth?** | Will it still work when you add rooms or properties? |
| **5. Does the vendor understand Indian hotels?** | Local compliance, support, OTA connections matter |
