## 🎯 10 Key Metrics Every Hotel Owner Should Track

Not all data is created equal. Focus on these **revenue-critical metrics**:

### 1. Occupancy Rate (%)
**What**: Percentage of rooms sold vs. total available  
**Why**: Baseline measure of demand  
**Action**: Adjust marketing spend when occupancy drops below target

### 2. Average Daily Rate (ADR)
**What**: Average revenue per room sold  
**Why**: Measures pricing effectiveness  
**Action**: Increase rates during high-demand periods

### 3. Revenue Per Available Room (RevPAR)
**What**: Occupancy × ADR  
**Why**: The gold standard for hotel performance  
**Action**: Track trends to identify growth opportunities

### 4. Direct Booking % 
**What**: Percentage of bookings from your website vs. OTAs  
**Why**: Direct bookings save 15-20% in commission  
**Action**: Invest in booking engine optimization when % is low

### 5. Channel Performance
**What**: Revenue and bookings by OTA/platform  
**Why**: Not all channels are equally profitable  
**Action**: Reallocate inventory to top-performing channels

### 6. Length of Stay (LOS)
**What**: Average nights per booking  
**Why**: Longer stays = lower turnover costs  
**Action**: Offer discounts for extended stays during low season

### 7. Booking Lead Time
**What**: Days between booking and check-in  
**Why**: Helps forecast demand and staffing  
**Action**: Launch last-minute promotions when lead time drops

### 8. Cancellation Rate
**What**: Percentage of bookings that cancel  
**Why**: High cancellations = revenue uncertainty  
**Action**: Adjust cancellation policies or require deposits

### 9. Guest Lifetime Value (LTV)
**What**: Total revenue from a guest over multiple stays  
**Why**: Retaining guests is 5x cheaper than acquiring new ones  
**Action**: Create loyalty programs for high-LTV segments

### 10. Room Turnaround Time
**What**: Average time from check-out to room-ready  
**Why**: Faster turnover = more same-day bookings  
**Action**: Optimize housekeeping workflows when time exceeds target

## 🚫 Common Analytics Mistakes Hotel Owners Make

| Mistake | Why It Hurts | Better Approach |
|---------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Tracking too many metrics** | Analysis paralysis | Focus on 5-10 revenue-critical KPIs |
| **Looking at data monthly** | Too late to act | Review daily/weekly for proactive decisions |
| **Ignoring direct booking data** | Missing profit opportunities | Track and optimize direct channel separately |
