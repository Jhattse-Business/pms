## 🔍 What Is a Channel Manager? (In Simple Terms)

A **Channel Manager** is a tool that connects your hotel's inventory and rates to multiple Online Travel Agencies (OTAs) like:
- Booking.com
- MakeMyTrip
- Agoda
- Expedia
- Goibibo
- Airbnb
- And 100+ more

**Think of it as your central command center**:  
Update a room rate or availability *once* in your system → the change instantly syncs across *all* connected OTAs.

No more manual updates. No more overbookings. No more rate parity headaches.
