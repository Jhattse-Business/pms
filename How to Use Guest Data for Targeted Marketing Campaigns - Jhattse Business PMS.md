## 🎯 How to Segment Guests for Targeted Campaigns

Segmentation is the key to effective targeted marketing. Here are the **most valuable guest segments** for Indian hotels:

### Segment #1: Repeat Guests

**Who**: Guests who have stayed 2+ times

**Why Target**: 5x more likely to book again than new guests

**Campaign Ideas**:
- "Welcome Back" exclusive rates (15-20% off)
- Loyalty tier upgrades (free breakfast, late checkout)
- Anniversary of first stay recognition

**Expected ROI**: 35-45% booking conversion

🔹 *Jhattse Feature*: Guest profile flags repeat visitors automatically with stay count and history.

---

### Segment #2: High-Value Guests

**Who**: Top 20% by total spend or ADR

**Why Target**: Disproportionate revenue contribution

**Campaign Ideas**:
- VIP early access to festival/wedding season bookings
- Complimentary room upgrades on future stays
- Personalized offers from owner/manager

**Expected ROI**: 40-50% booking conversion

🔹 *Jhattse Feature*: Guest segmentation by total spend, ADR, and booking frequency.

---

### Segment #3: Seasonal Travelers

**Who**: Guests who book during specific seasons (Diwali, summer, monsoon)

**Why Target**: Predictable travel patterns

**Campaign Ideas**:
- "Same Time Last Year" reminder campaigns
- Early-bird rates for their preferred season
- Package upgrades for returning seasonal guests

**Expected ROI**: 30-40% booking conversion

🔹 *Jhattse Feature*: Seasonal booking pattern analysis with automated campaign triggers.

---

### Segment #4: Occasion Travelers

**Who**: Guests who booked for anniversaries, birthdays, honeymoons

**Why Target**: Emotional connection, higher spend

**Campaign Ideas**:

**Expected ROI**: 40-55% booking conversion

## 📱 Using WhatsApp for Personalized Guest Marketing

In India, **WhatsApp is the most effective channel** for hotel marketing:

| Channel | Open Rate | Response Rate | Booking Conversion |
|---------|-----------|---------------|-------------------|
| Email | 20-25% | 3-5% | 2-4% |
| SMS | 45-50% | 5-8% | 3-5% |
| **WhatsApp** | **85-95%** | **25-35%** | **8-12%** |

### Best Practices for WhatsApp Marketing:

✅ **Get Permission First**: Only message guests who opted in  
✅ **Personalize Every Message**: Use guest name, reference past stays  
✅ **Keep It Concise**: 2-3 short paragraphs maximum  
✅ **Include Clear CTA**: Direct booking link with tracking  
✅ **Respect Timing**: Send during business hours (10 AM - 8 PM)  
✅ **Offer Easy Opt-Out**: "Reply STOP to unsubscribe"  

🔹 *Jhattse Feature*: WhatsApp Business API integration with guest data auto-population and opt-in management.

## 📈 Measuring Campaign Success: Key Metrics

Don't just send campaigns—**measure and optimize**:

| Metric | Formula | Good Benchmark |
|--------|---------|----------------|
| **Open Rate** | Opens ÷ Delivered | 35%+ (email), 85%+ (WhatsApp) |
| **Click-Through Rate** | Clicks ÷ Opens | 8%+ (email), 25%+ (WhatsApp) |
| **Conversion Rate** | Bookings ÷ Clicks | 5%+ (email), 10%+ (WhatsApp) |
| **Revenue Per Campaign** | Total booking value | Track per segment |
| **ROI** | (Revenue - Cost) ÷ Cost | 300%+ for targeted campaigns |
| **Unsubscribe Rate** | Unsubscribes ÷ Delivered | <2% (keep it low) |
| **Repeat Booking Rate** | Repeat bookings ÷ Total bookings | 35%+ for segmented campaigns |

## 🧭 Step-by-Step: Launching Your First Targeted Campaign

### ✅ Step 1: Audit Your Guest Data
- How many guest profiles do you have?
- What data fields are complete vs. empty?
- Which segments are largest (repeat, seasonal, occasion)?

🔹 *Jhattse Tip*: Use the "Guest Data Quality Report" to identify gaps.

---

### ✅ Step 2: Choose Your First Segment
Start with one high-value segment:
- **Best choice**: Repeat guests (highest conversion, easiest win)
- **Alternative**: Seasonal travelers (predictable timing)

🔹 *Jhattse Tip*: Don't try to segment everyone at once. Master one segment first.

---

### ✅ Step 3: Craft Your Offer
Make it compelling and exclusive:
- Discount: 15-20% off (not too deep, not too shallow)
- Value-add: Free breakfast, late checkout, room upgrade
- Urgency: Limited-time offer (7-14 days)

🔹 *Jhattse Tip*: Test value-adds vs. discounts—often similar conversion with higher margin.

---

### ✅ Step 4: Build Your Campaign
Use Jhattse campaign builder:
- Select guest segment
- Choose template (WhatsApp or email)
- Personalize with guest data fields
- Add tracking links for attribution

🔹 *Jhattse Tip*: Send a test message to yourself first to check formatting.

---

### ✅ Step 5: Launch and Monitor
- Send during optimal time (10 AM - 12 PM or 7 PM - 9 PM)
- Monitor open rates in first 24 hours
- Track bookings attributed to campaign

🔹 *Jhattse Tip*: Set up campaign alerts for real-time performance tracking.

### ✅ Step 6: Analyze and Optimize

## 🚫 Common Guest Data Marketing Mistakes to Avoid

| Mistake | Why It Hurts | Better Approach |
|---------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Buying email/phone lists** | Low quality, spam complaints, legal risk | Only market to guests who stayed with you |
| **Sending generic messages** | Low engagement, high unsubscribe | Personalize with guest name and stay history |
| **Messaging too frequently** | Guest fatigue, brand damage | Limit to 1-2 messages per month |
| **No clear opt-out option** | Frustration, potential violations | Always include "Reply STOP to unsubscribe" |
| **Ignoring data privacy** | Legal risk, trust erosion | Comply with DPDP Act 2023, get consent |
| **Not tracking results** | Can't improve what you don't measure | Track all campaign metrics in dashboard |
| **Using only one channel** | Missing guest preferences | Offer WhatsApp + email + SMS options |
| **Disconnected PMS and marketing** | Manual work, data errors | Choose integrated platform like Jhattse |
